1. Create cylinder.
2. Shrink the cylinder around all dimensions.
3. Shrink the cylinder in Z dimension.
4. Add a cone.
5. Shrink the cone in all dimensions.
6. Choose every 2. edge of the cone.
7. Slightly move them in all (mostly XY) dimensions to create unequality.
7. Randomly slect some faces of the cylinder.
8. Move them all slightly in all (mostly XY) dimensions to create unequality.
And we have low poly pinewood.
